About Me

I am a PhD candidate specializing in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Natural Language Processing (NLP), and scalable analytics. My work focuses on architecting hybrid computational frameworks that transform large-scale unstructured and multimodal data into structured, research-ready intelligence. I design end-to-end machine learning systems integrating transformer-based semantic modeling, information extraction, and advanced statistical learning methods. With expertise in predictive modeling, clustering, representation learning, and interactive data visualization, I develop decision-support tools that translate complex data into actionable insights across diverse application domains.
